# Unlocking your vault

## First time on this device

1. Open the vault and choose **Unlock Vault**.
2. If no PIN exists yet, create a PIN (at least 6 digits) and confirm it.
3. After unlock, your session stays active until you lock, vanish, or leave inactive.

## Daily unlock

You can unlock with:

1. **Passkey** — if you enrolled one on this device and your browser supports secure passkey encryption
2. **PIN** — always available as fallback

## Lock and vanish

- **Lock Now** clears your session keys from memory.
- **Vanish mode** briefly hides the vault UI when you switch away or after inactivity, then returns to locked state.

## If unlock fails

- Wrong PIN: try again carefully. ProofOrigin cannot reset your PIN.
- Passkey unavailable: use your PIN on this browser.
- Forgot PIN and no passkey or recovery kit: the vault may be unrecoverable on this device.

Never paste your PIN or recovery phrase into help chat or any website.
